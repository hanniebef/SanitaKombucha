Pineapple Kombucha

This refreshing pineapple kombucha combines the tropical flavor of the fruit with the probiotic benefits of fermentation. Perfect for those looking for a delicious and healthy drink, it’s ideal for relaxing moments. Enjoy it chilled and let each sip transport you to a paradisiacal beach.

Each serving provides 45 kcal, 0.5g protein, 10g carbohydrates (of which 8g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.01g salt.

Ginger Kombucha

This ginger kombucha offers a spicy and warm touch that makes it unique. Perfect for energizing yourself and enjoying its antioxidant properties, this drink is the ideal choice for any time of the day. Its distinctive flavor blends the best of tradition and modernity.

Each serving provides 40 kcal, 0.4g protein, 9g carbohydrates (of which 7g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.02g salt.

Pitahaya Kombucha

Exotic and vibrant, pitahaya kombucha is a feast for the senses. With its subtly sweet flavor and stunning natural color, this probiotic drink is as beautiful as it is beneficial. Perfect for adding a touch of adventure and health to your day.

Watermelon Kombucha

Refreshing and light, watermelon kombucha is the perfect companion for warm days. With a sweet and subtle flavor, this drink combines the probiotic benefits of kombucha with the summery touch of watermelon. Perfect to share and enjoy at any time.
