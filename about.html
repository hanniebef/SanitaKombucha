Pitahaya Kombucha

Exotic and vibrant, pitahaya kombucha is a feast for the senses. With its subtly sweet flavor and stunning natural color, this probiotic drink is as beautiful as it is beneficial. Perfect for adding a touch of adventure and health to your day.

Each serving provides 44 kcal, 0.4g protein, 9g carbohydrates (of which 7g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.02g salt.

Watermelon Kombucha

Refreshing and light, watermelon kombucha is the perfect companion for warm days. With a sweet and subtle flavor, this drink combines the probiotic benefits of kombucha with the summery touch of watermelon. Perfect to share and enjoy at any time.

Each serving provides 42 kcal, 0.4g protein, 10g carbohydrates (of which 8g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.01g salt.

Berry Kombucha

Full of flavor, this berry kombucha is a burst of sweetness and freshness. Made with a mix of berries like blueberries, blackberries, and raspberries, it’s the perfect drink for those who enjoy a fruity touch in their day. Ideal as a light and healthy companion.

Each serving provides 50 kcal, 0.3g protein, 11g carbohydrates (of which 9g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.02g salt.

Limon Kombucha

Lemon kombucha is a citrusy and vibrant option that stands out for its freshness. This classic flavor never goes out of style and is perfect for those seeking a tangy and revitalizing touch in their favorite probiotic drink. Ideal to pair with light meals or simply enjoy on its own.

Each serving provides 38 kcal, 0.3g protein, 8g carbohydrates (of which 6g are sugars), 0g fat, 0g fiber, and 0.03g salt.
